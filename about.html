ABOUT US

Laudo Nigeria Limited is a shipping, marine, logistics, oil, and gas services provider. As a licensed Nigerian Ports Authority agent and barge operator, we offer feeder vessel services, barge solutions, logistics, and transportation support to West African ports.

Our Mission

To bridge gaps in the shipping, marine, logistics, and oil & gas industries by delivering operations, maintenance, logistics, engineering, and project management services of the highest standard.

Corporate Values

Integrity

Resilience

We tirelessly pursue our goals, dedicated to delivering exemplary service.

Peace

We strive to provide peace and clarity in all our interactions, easing anxieties and fostering understanding.
