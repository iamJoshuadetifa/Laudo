Services

At Laudo Nigeria Limited, we offer a comprehensive range of Marine,shipping and logistics services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our services include shipping agency, barge operations, logistics support, and much more. With our deep understanding of the industry and our commitment to excellence, we ensure that all our services are delivered with the highest standards of professionalism and efficiency.

Shipping Agency

Our shipping agency services are tailored to ensure the smooth handling of your vessels at Nigerian and West African ports. We provide comprehensive support including port clearance, crew management and cargo handling of import and export.

Barge Operations

Our barge operations are designed to provide efficient transport solutions across the waterways of Nigeria and West Africa. We operate a fleet of barges capable of transporting various types of cargo with safety and reliability.

Logistics Support

Our logistics support services include the provision of transportation, warehousing, and supply chain management. We leverage our extensive network to deliver timely and cost-effective solutions for your logistics needs.

Offshore and Marine supply

We provide comprehensive supply services to both offshore and onshore oil rig installations. Our proven expertise extends to offshore deliveries for industry leaders, including NIMASA, Seabulk Offshore Operators Limited, Hydrodive Nigeria Limited, West Africa Offshore Limited, and Schlumberger Nigeria Limited.

Oil and Gas

Our experience in providing oil services, such as equipment leasing, dates back to 1999. We provide engineering procurement and construction(EPC)services, oil vessels, platform service vessels, offshore service vessels, barges. By being a one stop energy and services solution centre. We ensure the best of services.

Warehousing

Secure and dependable warehousing service are available, with technological equipments & storage designed to accommodate a variety of needs, Equipped with advanced inventory management, security and fire prevention systems to ensure optimal storage conditions and easy access for clients.

Bunkering Services

We offers professional and compliant bunkering services across Nigerian waters. Our bunkering operations adhere strictly to all industry standards and legal regulations to ensure quality, safety, and environmental responsibility in all our fuel transfer processes

Construction

We specialize in construction services for the maritime industry, including jetty and park construction, bollard installation, and other critical marine infrastructure. Our expert team ensures that each project meets the highest standards of safety and durability essential for coastal and offshore environments, delivering quality and resilience every step of the way
